Soft Wash Roof Cleaning

Protect your roof and boost curb appeal with professional soft wash roof cleaning in Broward County. We safely remove black streaks, algae, and mildew from tile, shingle, and metal roofs using low-pressure application and roof-safe detergents.

Pair your roof cleaning with house & building washing or driveway & paver cleaning for a complete refresh.

Why Soft Wash?

  • Low-pressure application prevents granule loss and damage
  • Targets algae (Gloeocapsa magma) and organic growth at the root
  • Improves energy efficiency by removing heat-trapping buildup
  • Helps meet HOA and insurance aesthetic requirements

Our Process

  • Pre-inspection of roof condition and flashing
  • Controlled chemical application with precise mix ratios
  • Low-pressure rinse; no aggressive blasting
  • Final walk-through and cleanup around landscaping
